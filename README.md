# Türkçe

## Proje Hakkında

**Gökbörü'nün Yapay Zekâ Sözlüğü**, çocukların Yapay Zekâ kavramlarını anlaşılır, güvenli, eleştirel ve etkileşimli biçimde öğrenmesi için hazırlanmış tarayıcı tabanlı bir eğitim portalıdır.

Proje; veri, algoritma, model, makine öğrenmesi, üretken Yapay Zekâ, prompt, ajan, etik, güvenlik, deepfake, kaynak kontrolü ve benzeri kavramları çocukların anlayabileceği bir dille açıklar.

Portalın rehber karakteri **Gökbörü**, çocuklara kavramları yalnızca tanım olarak değil; örnekler, mini etkinlikler, uyarı notları, eşleştirme oyunu ve kaynaklarla birlikte sunar.

# English

## About the Project

**Gökbörü's Artificial Intelligence Glossary** is a browser-based educational portal designed to help children learn Artificial Intelligence concepts in a clear, safe, critical and interactive way.

The project explains concepts such as data, algorithm, model, machine learning, generative AI, prompt, agent, ethics, safety, deepfake and source verification in child-friendly language.

The guide character **Gökbörü** introduces children to AI concepts not only through definitions, but also through examples, mini activities, warning notes, matching games and reliable educational resources.

This project is more than a static glossary. It aims to provide children with a visual, interactive and inquiry-based AI literacy experience.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this project is to help children understand, question and safely use Artificial Intelligence concepts rather than simply memorizing them.

The portal aims to support the following skills:

- Learning AI concepts in child-friendly language
- Understanding the relationship between data, algorithms, models and prediction
- Using generative AI tools more consciously
- Developing prompt-writing and clear-question skills
- Building source-checking and verification habits
- Recognizing deepfake, misinformation and bias risks
- Developing awareness of safe and ethical AI use
- Reinforcing concepts through mini activities

## Portal Sections

### 1. Introduction / Hero Area

The portal introduces the AI glossary world through the Gökbörü character and summarizes the purpose, concept scope and learning approach.

### 2. Glossary

The main section where children can search, filter and read Artificial Intelligence concepts.

Glossary cards include:

- Term name
- Category
- Level
- Child-friendly definition
- Concrete example
- Mini activity
- Safety or attention note

### 3. Search and Filtering

The search area is designed to tolerate Turkish character variations. For example, typing “zeka” can still find “Yapay Zekâ”.

Filtering options include:

- Category
- Level
- Sorting
- Search text

### 4. Matching Game

A mini reinforcement game that helps children match AI concepts with their meanings.

### 5. Mini Labs

Short activity areas that encourage children to think actively about AI concepts.

Example activities:

- News Detective
- Prompt Master
- Data Hunter
- Deepfake Alert
- Ethical Designer
- Safe Use Checklist

### 6. Resources

The content framework of the portal is based on child-focused Artificial Intelligence and AI literacy studies by institutions such as UNICEF, UNESCO, OECD, MIT and Common Sense Media.

This section brings together reliable references for teachers, librarians, parents and students.

### 7. Concept Card PDF / Print

The portal is designed so that only the selected concept card can be printed or saved as a PDF, rather than printing the entire page.

This feature helps teachers, librarians and workshop facilitators prepare concept-based handouts.
